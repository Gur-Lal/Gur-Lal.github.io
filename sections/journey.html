2. User Journey Mapping

Thought process behind Margaret Atwood's user journey map


The main actor of this user journey map is Margaret Atwood. I chose a very high-level scope for Margaret's user journey map, since that was the most aligned with her goals. It starts at Margaret's motivation in the product and ends at the user's long-term adoption of the product. I took into consideration Margaret's actions, key touchpoints, emotions, potential pain points, and potential solutions at each stage.


Thought process behind Juan Diego's user journey map


The main actor of this user journey map is Juan Diego. I chose a lower-level scope for Juan's user journey map, since he's predominantly concerned about his sporadic flare-ups. It starts at Juan's concern for a symptom and ends at his response to his provider's feedback. Similar to Margaret, I took into consideration Juan's actions, key touchpoints, emotions, potential pain points, and potential solutions at each stage.
